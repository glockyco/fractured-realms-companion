# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Fractured Realms players using the companion inside the running game on Windows, Linux, or macOS. They need to identify items, understand sources and uses, resolve crafting dependencies, and start the next valid game action without leaving play or managing the native action queue.

## Product Purpose

Provide a local, build-matched item wiki and direct-action planner derived from the player's own game files. Success means a player can move from an unknown item or desired quantity to a clear, executable dependency plan in seconds, while every automated step remains visible, interruptible, and faithful to live game state.

## Brand Personality

Precise, calm, capable. The companion should feel trustworthy under dense information and quiet during active play. Language is direct, operational, and specific.

## Anti-references

Mobile-game HUDs: oversized glowing controls, attention-hungry badges, currency clutter, ornamental meters, and effects that compete with the game. The companion must not imitate a separate website or generic marketing surface.

## Design Principles

1. **Stay inside the task.** Surface the next useful fact or action without sending the player elsewhere.
2. **Make automation legible.** Show dependency order, blockers, current targets, progress, and stop controls before asking for trust.
3. **Prefer live truth.** Game state and build-extracted data outrank cached assumptions.
4. **Earn density.** Compact information is valuable when hierarchy, labels, and keyboard flow remain clear.
5. **Disappear when idle.** The overlay supports play and never competes with it.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. Support complete keyboard navigation, visible focus, 4.5:1 body-text contrast, non-color status cues, readable zoom, and reduced-motion alternatives. Do not encode blocked, warning, active, or complete states by color alone.
